Age Calculation Methods

Western System

Age increases exactly on birthdays. For example: 3 years and 11 months = 3 years until next birthday.

Traditional Chinese System

Newborns start at age 1, with age increasing at Chinese New Year rather than birthdays.

Month Calculation Note

When calculating between month-ends (e.g., Feb 28 - Mar 31), we use same-day-of-month calculation (Feb 28 - Mar 28 = 1 month).
